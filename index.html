About me

I'm a PhD candidate in computer science at Université Laval. I study under the supervision of Claude-Guy Quimper and Jonathan Gaudreault. I'm part of the PPC laboratory at Université Laval. I'm also a FORAC and CIRRELT student.

Research interests

My reasearch interests include constraint programming and parallelism. More specifically, I work on search strategy paraellization for problems defined by constraints.
